Chicken Adobo

Enjoy the classic Filipino Chicken Adobo with this simple recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for about 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, combine marinated chicken, vinegar, onion, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  3. Simmer over medium heat until chicken is cooked and sauce thickens.
  4. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
